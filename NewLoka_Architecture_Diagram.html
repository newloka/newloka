// Key Hierarchy

DMK — Device Master Keyhardware keystore + Argon2id passphrase. Never leaves device.

PRK — Patient Record KeyOne per patient. Encrypted under DMK. Unlocked per session.

SSK — Sync Session KeyEphemeral. X25519 per session. Forward secrecy.

ALK — Audit Log KeyEd25519 signing only. Cannot decrypt anything.
